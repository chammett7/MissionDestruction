Save the air we breathe before our world is filled with poisonous gas.


AIR


You've been dropped in the middle of a mission zone where the air is highly polluted. You have a mask and goggles to wear as you make your way through the smoggy cloudiness. The goggles are fogging up your vision, and it is hard to breathe through the mask. Your skin feels like its burning and as you look around you ask yourself, how could this be? What should you do?
