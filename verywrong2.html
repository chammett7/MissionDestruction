So Wrong...

Air pollution shortens the average lifespan by 1-2 years. It is tiny particles mixed together in the atmosphere. Each individual molecule is impossible to see with the naked eye; however when trillions gather you see them as a haze over the area. By taking your mask off you exposed all of your vital organs to high-level pollution. Within minutes your lungs begin to feel heavy, and you have to call for backup. Mission Terminated.
